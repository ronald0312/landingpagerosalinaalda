About Us

Welcome to Tabe Nine Resto

Table Nine Kitchen is one of the famous and best restaurants in the Purwokerto area. Located on Jl. Prof. Dr. HR. Bunyamin, Pabuaran, North Purwokerto. Spacious restaurant with outdoor seating, serving Indonesian dishes, Chinese food, food packages, hot pots and others. The best menus are all here.

Table Nine Kitchen carries the concept of Cafe & Resto, as well as a relaxed arrangement of places, perfect for being used as a place to hang out and hold various events. Besides that, the view and design of the dining room arrangement is very instagrammable. While waiting for the food to arrive, we can take time to take photos at the interesting spots there.
